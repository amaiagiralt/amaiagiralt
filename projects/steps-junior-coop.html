Steps junior Coop

Creating a cooperative with 17 years

After doing a little casting I finally entered to the university…we were 30 in total. Do you remember how was your first week at the university? I do.

“Okay guys you have to build a company for the following 4 years and well each of you must get 10.000€ benefits and 15.000€ invoicing (at least) working in real projects, you must be the creator or those projects”. And this is how all begins.

It was all about learning by doing. 15 people putting all our hearts to run a profitable business. We start with little things but ey we end up developing different business lines in the fields of gastronomy, arts,education, human centered innovation, event management and travel. It was all about launching new business ideas and developing them. With the money we got we invested again in our projects and we travel through Finland, San Francisco, China and India.

In order to achieve our goals and learn, team work was crucial. Twice per week we gather for 4 hours to share new learnings, improve our personal skills and discuss about our vision,values…
