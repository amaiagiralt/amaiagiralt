Grutrip

Group trips easily organized

PROJECT OVERVIEW

The problem:

When you are about to organize a trip with a big group of friends is always tough to agree where to go, when to go and so many details

The solution:

Grutrip helps you organizing your next trip with an easy and agile voting system. In addition, it gives you new ideas for your next destination.

My rol:

Product designer & UX-UI Designer (personal project)

Tools:

Typeform (Surveys), Pen & paper, Sketch (Wireframes & Visual design), Invision (Prototype), Principle (UI Animations and Microinteractions).
