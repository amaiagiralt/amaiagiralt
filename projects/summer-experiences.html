Summer experiences

Tourist activities agency

It was summer, me and my colleagues were all from San Sebastian...we knew every place in the city we knew where to have fun, where you should go to eat, we knew also what you need to live to remember the experience and we knew that lots of foreigners come to San Sebastian to practice Spanish. For that reason we created different cultural activities to show the city and help them improve their spanish. For this purpose we collaborate with different language schools.
