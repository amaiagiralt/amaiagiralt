Koxk

Social event

Koxk is a event that gather gastronomy and solidarity. In the neighborhood of Intxaurrondo (San Sebastian- Basque Country) four areas are organized for the celebration of different gastronomic activities. 

The goal of Koxk is to raise money and get support for the project “Sabores”

What is “Sabores” project?

The Sabores project is a solidarity-gastronomic project whose objective is to reverse the chronic food insecurity suffered by the population of Wukro and the region of Tigray (Ethiopia).

To guarantee a long term impact this project will guarantee the sustainability of the Saint Mary College and will develop the agri-food industry in the region.
